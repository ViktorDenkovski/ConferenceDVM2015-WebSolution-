Drim Hotel**** Congress Hall
Hotel & Conference Center


Hotel Drim**** is located on the Shore of Ohrid Lake, at the beginning of river Crn Drim and the city quay promenade that runs through the town, 300 m from the center of town Struga.

Rooms’ friendly décor in a spacious pleasant atmosphere is designed to suit every taste. Warm, with comfortable beds and appointed with numerous amenities, these rooms are the height of this marvellous experience. A stay at Hotel Drim will give you the vibrant feeling, choosing from a variety of room choices from standard to superior or deluxe, and up to spacious family suites. Each room has its’ own unique style and character, and very few of the rooms are the same. All 200 rooms have been carefully designed to incorporate comfort and style bearing in mind that our guests can be here for both business and pleasure.

The interior has been cleverly designed, taking into consideration even the smallest of details to ensure maximum guest enjoyment. You will find the best quality linens and towels, comfortable furniture, modern utilities with different options so that you can create the atmosphere you desire.
