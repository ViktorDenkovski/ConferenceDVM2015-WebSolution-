Dear colleagues,

It is our privilege to extend to all of you our cordial invitation to participate in the 6th International Scientific Meeting DAYS OF VETERINARY MEDICINE – 2015.

For the 6th time the Faculty of Veterinary Medicine of the Ss. Cyril and Methodius University in Skopje will gladly host scientists, veterinary practitioners and official veterinarians on a meeting that should incorporate science into every day veterinary practice.

This year, 24th to 26th September is the period, the pretty town of Struga is the place and Hotel Drim is the exact location of the meeting.

The meeting will retain the usual popular format, including a number of invited plenary lectures, short presentations and inspiring workshops. The scientific program will comprise a number of professionally delivered presentations, selected by the Committee based on scientific importance and novelty on the following topics: animal health, welfare and reproduction, as well as public health and food safety.

But that’s not all! We’ll give out best to organize what’s left of your time after the lectures offering a bit of nature, a bit of history and a lot of culinary experience.

We look forward to seeing you this year in Struga!

The Organizing Committee,
